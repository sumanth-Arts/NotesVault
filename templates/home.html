About the Portal

NotesVault was created to make studying simple, fast, and stress-free for students.
It brings semester-wise notes, unit-wise concepts, PPTs, and question banks into one place for quick learning and easy revision. The notes are short, clear, and exam-focused, helping students prepare efficiently—even at the last moment.
This platform was designed and developed by Sumanth, a B.Tech CSIT student, with the goal of helping students learn smarter and score better.
Built by a student, for students.
